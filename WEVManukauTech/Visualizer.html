Visualizer

Try it yourself!

Try playing around with frequencies and loudness. When you click the Start button, a sine wave will play using the frequency and loudness you have entered. The color of the circle will change based on the frequency you choose. The size and position of the circle will be completely random. If you enter anything other than a number into the "Frequency" field, the circle created will be black and no sound will play. Note: You will probably not be able to hear sound around the 20Hz and 20,000Hz frequencies, as these are the limits of human hearing.
